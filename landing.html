AcadEx was created for the AFRETEC UNILAG Inclusive Education Innovation Challenge 2026, themed "Digitally Enabled Inclusive Education — Expanding Access, Equity, and Opportunity for All."

We are two 100 Level students at UNILAG. We built AcadEx because we live this problem every day. When we submitted our first CBT exam, we couldn't see what we answered. We couldn't challenge a wrong mark. We couldn't find the right materials without digging through WhatsApp groups. AcadEx is our answer to that.
